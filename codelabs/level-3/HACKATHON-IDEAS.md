## 🎯 How to Choose YOUR Winning Idea

### The 5-Question Filter:

1. **Can I build an MVP in 5-6 hours?**
   - ✅ Single core feature working well
   - ❌ Five features half-working

2. **Does it solve a problem I've personally experienced?**
   - ✅ Authentic passion shows in demos
   - ❌ Generic "someone might need this"

3. **Can a 5-year-old understand my demo?**
   - ✅ "AI sees food, suggests recipe"
   - ❌ "Multi-modal embeddings with RAG architecture..."

4. **Would I use this tomorrow?**
   - ✅ Practical daily utility
   - ❌ "Cool tech demo" with no real use

5. **Does it make people say "wow, I need this!"?**
   - ✅ Emotional reaction in demo
   - ❌ Polite nods from judges

---

### Judge-Winning Formula:

**IMPACT × INNOVATION × EXECUTION × DEMO**

- **Impact (30%)**: Who does this help? How many people?
- **Innovation (30%)**: What's unique about your approach?
- **Execution (20%)**: Does it actually work smoothly?
- **Demo (20%)**: Did you make me feel something?

**Pro Tip:** A simple idea executed perfectly beats a complex idea half-done.

---

### Red Flags to Avoid:

❌ **"It's like Uber but for..."** - Too derivative  
❌ **"We'll use blockchain..."** - Buzzword overload  
❌ **"In phase 2 we'll add..."** - Focus on NOW  
❌ **"This could work for everyone!"** - Too broad  
❌ **"The market is huge..."** - Vague value prop  

✅ **"My grandma can't read medicine labels. This helps her."** - Specific, emotional, clear
