# Our Story

> *One hacker onsite. One remote. One AI that watches.*

---

Abdul Rasheed was in the room — the hum of New York, whiteboards, other teams' keyboards.  
Mehanaz MI was hundreds of miles away, on a screen.

Same deadline. Same problem. Different rooms.

---

**The problem:** Every presenter is blind to themselves. The filler words. The energy drop. The back row that checked out five minutes ago. Feedback only comes after — if at all.

**The idea:** What if the AI just *watched*? No prompts. No uploads. Just camera, mic, and real-time intelligence.

We pointed Gemini Live at the speaker. Then at the audience. Two angles. One dashboard updating live.

---

It didn't come easy. SDK versions broke silently. Auth tokens leaked from the shell. Error `1008` haunted us for hours.

But when it finally clicked — audio flowing, scores appearing, coaching tips whispering back — we realized:

**The AI had coached us through building itself.**
